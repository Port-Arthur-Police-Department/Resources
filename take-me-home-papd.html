About the Take-Me-Home-PAPD Registry: This program helps first responders quickly identify and assist vulnerable individuals who may wander or become lost due to various conditions. By providing this information in advance, officers can better communicate with and support your loved one during an emergency. All information is confidential and used only for police response purposes.
